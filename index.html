Crystal Collector

You will be given random number at the start f the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amout of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your toatl score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal id hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
